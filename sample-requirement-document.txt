Requirement Document - vDSR Deployment

Customer: Global Telco
vDSR Version: 24.2.0
Platform: Openstack
Openstack Version: Yoga

Project Overview:
This document outlines the requirements for deploying Oracle's vDSR for Global Telco.

Deployment Details:
- The deployment will consist of 2 sites for geo-redundancy.
- Each site will have one NOAM, for a total of 2 NOAMs.
- A spare SOAM is not required for this deployment.
- SBR (Subscriber Binding Repository) is required.
- IDIH (Integrated Diameter Intelligence Hub) is required.
- UDR (User Data Repository) is not required.
